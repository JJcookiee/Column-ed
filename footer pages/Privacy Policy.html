Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Column-ed respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we do and do not collect when you use our website.

Information We Collect

Column-ed does not collect real personal data.

Any usernames, reviews, ratings, lists, or other content shown on the site are not linked to real individuals and are used only to provide the sites core features.

Please do not enter any real data

How we use Information

Information displayed on Column-Ed is used only to:

  • Allow users to log media they have watched or read
  • Display reviews, ratings, and media lists
  • Enable basic site functionality

Cookies

Column-ed does not use tracking or advertising cookies.

If cookies are used, they are strictly necessary for basic site functionality, such as maintaining a login session.

Third-Party Services

Column-ed does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

Media information (such as titles, images, or descriptions) may come from external sources, but no user data is sent to those services.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the limited information used by the site. No sensitive or personal data is stored.
